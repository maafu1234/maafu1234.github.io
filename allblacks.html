New Zealand All Blacks

The All Blacks are the most successful sports franchise in history. They have won three World Cups and won more than three-quarters of the matches they have played in their 125-year history, more than any major national sports team. Many New Zealand fans go to rugby matches wondering not whether their team will win, but rather by how much.
