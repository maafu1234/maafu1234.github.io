Arizona State University

Even when things get rough, the Sun Devil are always alive. They love their team, even when it reaches 200 degrees outside. Also their student section is one of the most wildest sections between almost every other D1 schools.

Sparky

Sparky the Sun Devil, ASU’s mascot, is one of the University’s most beloved symbols. Sparky enhances the fan's experience, by being a consistent public figure who is there to have a good time. He loves to make people laugh and smile and he can be humorous at times, so being a character really relates to the fan's energy. Being able to be see him live is unspeakably cool.
